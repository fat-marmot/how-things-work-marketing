Privacy & terms.

The plain-English version is on this page. The full legal text follows. They say the same thing — we tried to make sure of that.

Effective · April 1, 2026 · v3.2

Overview

We try to know as little about you as possible. The app works without an account, stores your reading history on your device, and never sells data to anyone.

How {things} Work is operated by How {things} Work Ltd., a company registered in Scotland (company no. SC712304). We are the data controller for any personal information described in this notice. You can reach us at privacy@howthingswork.app.

What we collect

The app is designed to function with the smallest viable footprint of personal data. In practice we collect three things, all of them limited and purposeful.

Diagnostic crash reports

If the app crashes, an anonymised crash report is sent so we can fix it. Reports include the device model, the OS version, and a stack trace. They do not contain your reading history, IP address, or any identifier that ties the report to you. You can turn this off in Settings › Privacy.

Newsletter subscriptions

If you sign up for the Sunday newsletter, we keep your email address and the subscription date. We use one provider — Buttondown — to send the email. You can unsubscribe in one tap from any issue, which deletes the record entirely.

Voluntary correspondence

When you write to us via email or the contact form, we keep the message and your reply address as long as needed to handle the conversation, and for up to one year afterwards.

What we don't collect

No accounts, no analytics SDKs, no advertising, no behavioural tracking.

The app does not require an account. It does not contain advertising or third-party tracking SDKs. We do not record which topics you read, when you read them, how long you spend, or what you search for. None of that data leaves your device.

Your reading history, bookmarks, and preferences are stored locally on the device. If you sign in to iCloud Drive, those files may be backed up by Apple under their terms; we never see them.

Third parties we rely on

The bare minimum, all reputable, all listed:

  • Apple — App Store distribution, in-app purchase processing for the donation tier.
  • Cloudflare — content delivery for app updates and the website.
  • Buttondown — newsletter delivery, only if you subscribe.
  • Stripe — recurring donation processing, only if you choose to donate. Card details never reach our servers.

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or any other behavioural analytics service.

Your rights

Under the UK GDPR and the EU GDPR, you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, correct it, delete it, or ask us to stop processing it. Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have analogous rights including the right to know and the right to delete. We do not sell personal information, so the right to opt out of sale is not applicable.

To exercise any of these rights, write to privacy@howthingswork.app. We respond within thirty days, usually within a week.

Children

The app is rated 9+ and is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under thirteen. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this notice

If we make material changes to how we handle your data, we will note the change here, bump the version above, and — for newsletter subscribers — say so in the next issue.
